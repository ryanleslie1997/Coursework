Edinburgh Escape Game

You are stuck in Edinburgh castle and there are lots of zombies coming after him. He will need to find a way out soon or he might get bitten and become a zombie. Select option 1 to go out the window, option 2 to block the door with a chair or option 3 to hide and hope they don't see you
